Bean Salad

Recipe by chemjo



Description

This salad is a delicious combination of five (5) types of beans.


Ingredients

  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can black beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can green beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can wax beans, drained
  • ½ cup chopped green pepper
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup salad oil
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Combine garbanzo beans, kidney beans,
    black beans, green beans, wax beans,
    green pepper, onion, and celery in a
    large bowl; toss to mix.

  3. Whisk together sugar, oil, vinegar,
    salt, and pepper in a separate bowl
    until sugar is dissolved; pour over
    bean mixture. Mix well.

  4. Refrigerate salad for 8 hours to
    overnight before serving.
